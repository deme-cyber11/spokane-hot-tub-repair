Professional Spa Repair Services in Spokane

Spas and hot tubs share a lot of the same technology, but spa systems often have unique configurations that require specialized knowledge to repair correctly. At Inland NW Hot Tubs, our technicians are experienced with the full range of portable spas, in-ground spas, and swim spas found in homes across Spokane County. Whether your spa's jets have lost pressure, the control panel is displaying error codes, or you're dealing with persistent plumbing leaks, we have the diagnostic tools and replacement parts to resolve the problem efficiently. Most spa repairs are completed in a single visit, getting you back to relaxation without a long wait.

Spa Systems We Repair

A modern spa is a complex system with several interdependent components. Here are the most common spa repair services we provide to Spokane homeowners:

  • Jet systems — Weak jets, stuck jets, air locks, and diverter valve failures. We repair or replace individual jet bodies, nozzles, and the plumbing that feeds them.
  • Plumbing repairs — Cracked fittings, loose unions, deteriorated gaskets, and manifold leaks. We trace every leak to its source and make a permanent repair.
  • Control panels and circuit boards — Error codes, unresponsive buttons, display failures, and intermittent shutdowns. We troubleshoot and replace Balboa, Gecko, and other major control systems.
  • Electrical components — GFCI breaker trips, relay failures, wiring issues, and transformer problems. All electrical work is performed safely by our qualified technicians.
  • Water chemistry and sanitation — Persistent cloudy water, strong chemical odor, or foam that won't go away can point to a failing ozonator, UV sanitizer, or a circulation issue that no amount of chemicals will fix.
