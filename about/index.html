Rooted in the Inland Northwest Community

Inland NW Hot Tubs was built on a simple idea: Spokane homeowners deserve fast, honest hot tub service without the runaround. For over eight years, we've shown up on time, diagnosed problems correctly the first time, and delivered repairs that last — because repeat business only comes from doing the job right.

Our technicians train on the latest equipment from brands like Hot Spring, Jacuzzi, Sundance, Caldera, and Bullfrog. We invest in continuing education so we're never guessing when we open the equipment bay on your spa. That expertise translates directly into faster diagnosis times and fewer return visits — saving you money and frustration.

We understand that Spokane winters are unforgiving. A broken heater in January isn't an inconvenience — it's urgent. That's why we keep our schedules flexible and stock the most commonly needed replacement parts in our service vehicles. In most cases, we can complete the repair during the initial visit rather than leaving you waiting on a parts order.

From Airway Heights to Liberty Lake, from Cheney to Post Falls, our team is proud to serve the full Spokane metro area. When you call Inland NW Hot Tubs, you're talking to a local technician who knows the region and cares about the community — not a national franchise call center. We're your neighbors, and we treat every job like it's our own backyard.
