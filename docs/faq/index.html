Everything you need to know about hot tub repair in Spokane.

Hot tub repair in Spokane typically ranges from $150 to $1,500 depending on the issue. Common repairs: heating element replacement ($200–$400), pump repair ($300–$600), control board replacement ($400–$800), jet replacement ($150–$300). Diagnostic visit is included with any repair.
