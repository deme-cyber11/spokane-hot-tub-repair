About Spokane Hot Tub Repair

Trusted hot tub repair professionals serving Spokane County for over 8 years.

Your Local Hot Tub Repair Experts

With 8+ years of hands-on experience, Spokane Hot Tub Repair has become the go-to service provider for homeowners across Spokane County. We specialize in diagnosing and repairing all types of hot tubs and spas, getting your equipment back to peak performance quickly and affordably.

We service all major brands including Jacuzzi, Hot Spring, Sundance, Caldera, Bullfrog, Master Spa, Marquis, and more. Whether it's a faulty heater, worn-out pump, cracked shell, or a control board issue, our technicians have seen it all and fixed it all.

From Spokane to Spokane Valley, Liberty Lake, Cheney, and beyond, we bring professional-grade repair right to your backyard. Every job is backed by our commitment to quality workmanship and transparent pricing.
