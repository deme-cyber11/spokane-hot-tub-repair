Common Hot Tub Problems We Fix

Hot tubs are complex systems with electrical, plumbing, and mechanical components that all need to work together. Here are the most common issues Spokane homeowners call us about:

  • Heating problems — hot tub not reaching temperature, slow heating, or no heat at all. Often caused by failed heating elements, faulty thermostats, or high-limit sensor trips.
  • Pump failures — weak water flow, loud humming or grinding noises, or a pump that won't turn on. Could be motor bearings, a bad capacitor, or a seized impeller.
  • Leaks — water pooling beneath the cabinet, dropping water levels, or wet equipment. We trace leaks to the exact fitting, union, or seal and repair it properly.
  • Electrical issues — tripped breakers, error codes on the topside panel, or a hot tub that won't power on. We diagnose control boards, relays, and wiring safely.
  • Jet malfunctions — jets stuck open or closed, weak pressure, or air in the lines. We repair or replace jet bodies, diverter valves, and plumbing connections.
  • Water chemistry problems — cloudy water, foaming, or persistent odor that chemicals can't resolve. Sometimes the root cause is a failing ozonator or UV sanitizer.
